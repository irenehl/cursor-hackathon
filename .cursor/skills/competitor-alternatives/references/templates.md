# Comparison Page Templates

Detailed templates for each page format.

## Format 1: [Competitor] Alternative (Singular)

### Meta Tags
```yaml
title: "[Competitor] Alternative: [Your Product] | [Year] Comparison"
description: "Looking for a [Competitor] alternative? [Your Product] offers [key differentiator]. Compare features, pricing, and see why teams switch."
```

### Page Structure Template

```markdown
# [Competitor] Alternative: [Your Product]

## TL;DR
[2-3 sentences: key differences, why switch, who should consider]

## Why People Look for [Competitor] Alternatives

[Common pain points - 2-3 paragraphs]
- Pain point 1 with context
- Pain point 2 with context
- Pain point 3 with context

## [Your Product]: The [Competitor] Alternative

[Positioning paragraph - why you're a strong alternative]

### Key Advantages Over [Competitor]
- Advantage 1: [explanation]
- Advantage 2: [explanation]
- Advantage 3: [explanation]

## Detailed Comparison

### Features

**[Your Product]**
- Feature description
- How it works
- Use cases

**[Competitor]**
- Feature description
- How it works
- Limitations

**Bottom line**: [When each is better]

### Pricing

| Tier | [Your Product] | [Competitor] | Difference |
|------|----------------|--------------|------------|
| Starter | $X/mo | $Y/mo | [difference] |
| Pro | $X/mo | $Y/mo | [difference] |
| Enterprise | Custom | Custom | [difference] |

**Hidden costs to consider**:
- [Your Product]: [any hidden costs]
- [Competitor]: [any hidden costs]

### Support & Service

[Paragraph comparison of support quality, response times, resources]

### Ease of Use

[Paragraph comparison of onboarding, learning curve, UX]

### Integrations

[Comparison of integration ecosystem]

## Who Should Switch to [Your Product]

[Clear criteria - 2-3 paragraphs]
- Ideal for: [use cases]
- Best if: [conditions]
- Not ideal if: [honest limitations]

## Who Should Stay with [Competitor]

[Honest assessment - builds trust]
- Better if: [scenarios]
- Consider staying if: [conditions]

## Migration Path

### What Transfers
- [Data/features that migrate easily]

### What Needs Reconfiguration
- [Items requiring setup]

### Migration Support
- [What help you provide]
- [Timeline]
- [Resources available]

## What Customers Say

> "[Testimonial from switcher]" - [Name], [Title], [Company]

> "[Another testimonial]" - [Name], [Title], [Company]

## Ready to Switch?

[CTA section with clear next steps]
```
