# Marketing Ideas by Category

Complete list of 139 marketing ideas organized by category with descriptions and implementation guidance.

## Content & SEO (Ideas 1-10)

### 1. Easy Keyword Ranking
Target low-competition keywords your audience searches for. Use tools like Ahrefs or SEMrush to find keywords with search volume but low difficulty.

**Implementation:**
- Identify 10-20 low-competition keywords
- Create focused content for each
- Optimize for featured snippets
- Build internal links

**Expected outcome:** Organic traffic from long-tail searches

---

### 2. Glossary Marketing
Create comprehensive glossary pages for industry terms. These rank well and capture high-intent searches.

**Implementation:**
- List 50-100 industry terms
- Write clear definitions with examples
- Link to related content
- Update quarterly

**Expected outcome:** Authority building, backlinks, organic traffic

---

### 3. Content Repurposing
Turn one piece of content into multiple formats (blog → video → podcast → social posts).

**Implementation:**
- Start with pillar content
- Extract quotes for social
- Create video summaries
- Turn into email series

**Expected outcome:** 5-10x content output from same effort

---

### 4. Programmatic SEO
Create thousands of pages from templates using data (locations, tools, use cases).

**Implementation:**
- Identify scalable template (e.g., "Best tools for [use case]")
- Build data source
- Generate pages programmatically
- Monitor and optimize

**Expected outcome:** Massive scale of SEO pages

---

### 5. Topic Clusters
Group related content around pillar topics with internal linking.

**Implementation:**
- Identify 3-5 pillar topics
- Create 10-20 supporting articles per pillar
- Link structure: supporting → pillar
- Update regularly

**Expected outcome:** Improved rankings, better user experience

---

### 6. Answer Engine Optimization
Optimize for AI search results (ChatGPT, Perplexity, etc.).

**Implementation:**
- Write clear, concise answers
- Use structured data
- Focus on accuracy and citations
- Test queries in AI tools

**Expected outcome:** Visibility in AI search results

---

### 7. Long-Form Guides

**Implementation:**

- Research thoroughly
- Include examples, templates, tools
- Update annually

**Expected outcome:** Authority, backlinks, evergreen traffic

### 8. Case Study Library

**Expected outcome:** Social proof, sales enablement, SEO
